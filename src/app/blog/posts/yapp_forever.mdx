## What is Yapp Forever?

Yapp Forever is a background voice-capture system.

You press a hotkey, speak your thoughts, and at the end of the day everything is:
- transcribed
- cleaned
- structured into readable notes

The goal is simple: remove friction between thinking and recording.

Typing kills flow. This doesn’t.

---

## Why I built it

Most thoughts don’t survive long enough to be written down.

Especially when:
- you’re reading
- watching something
- walking
- or just thinking

This tool exists to:
- capture thoughts instantly
- preserve them without effort
- turn them into something usable later

---

## How it works

The system runs in the background and follows a simple pipeline:

1. You press a hotkey and speak  
2. Audio is recorded throughout the day  
3. At night:
   - transcription happens (via Groq / Whisper)
   - cleanup + structuring happens (via Claude)
4. Output is saved as clean markdown notes

---

## Core idea: “New Session”

The most important concept in the system:

You can segment your thoughts by saying:

> “New session — [topic]”

Example:

- “New session — Lex Fridman podcast”
- “Next session — AI regulation article”

This creates structured sections in your final notes instead of one messy blob.

---

## Daily workflow

Typical usage looks like this:

**During the day**
- Press hotkey → speak → pause  
- Repeat whenever needed  
- Use session markers to separate topics  

**At night**
- System processes everything automatically  

**Next morning**
- Clean notes are ready  

---

## What you get as output

Each day produces a markdown file with:

### 1. Cleaned notes
- filler words removed  
- grammar fixed  
- structure added  
- your voice preserved  

### 2. Flags section
- shows what AI changed  
- marks major vs minor edits  
- keeps everything transparent  

---

## Stack behind it

- **Groq (Whisper)** → transcription  
- **Claude (Anthropic)** → cleanup + structuring  
- **Gemini** → fallback  
- **Markdown files** → storage  
- **Obsidian (optional)** → reading + querying
